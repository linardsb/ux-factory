## Summary
Built the standalone deep-linkable handoff-pack viewer: a pure view-time ES module (`system/handoff-viewer.mjs`) that joins the committed `pack.json` ↔ `vocabulary.json` by component name and renders, per component, three projections of one generated source **side by side** — the machine spec head, the engineer prose docs, and the agent-vocabulary entry — driven by a bare page (`handoff.html`). Added a zero-dep deterministic bundle generator (`agent-layer/gen-pack-bundle.mjs`) emitting one committed `pack.bundle.json` (every pack file inlined as a UTF-8 string), wired into `build.mjs` and `drift-check.mjs`, and surfaced as a plain `<a download>` on the page. The one-source claim is made structurally (column adjacency) with zero pedagogy callouts.
